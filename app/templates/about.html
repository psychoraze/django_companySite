Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company based in Palo Alto, California.

Founded in 2003, Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Tesla designs and manufactures electric vehicles, as well as energy storage and solar products.

The company is known for its innovative technology, including the Autopilot advanced driver-assistance system and its Gigafactories for battery production.
